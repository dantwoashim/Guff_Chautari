# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a harassment-free, practical, and respectful collaboration environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give technical feedback on code and behavior, not on people.
- Be direct, but not insulting.
- Respect project constraints (security, safety, reliability).

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews.
- Sharing private/security-sensitive data without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors who violate this code.

## Scope
This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related community spaces.

## Reporting
Report violations to project maintainers through private channels. Provide links and evidence when possible.
